PDFyre is a free browser-based PDF tool suite. OCR, Merge, Split, Compress, Rotate, PDF to Images and Images to PDF — all tools run entirely in your browser. No file uploads, no account required, free forever. Your files never leave your device. All processing happens locally using JavaScript in your browser tab, powered by PDF.js, Tesseract.js and pdf-lib.
